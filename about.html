About Us

Hooked On You is a passionate crochet store based in Coimbatore, dedicated to bringing you high-quality crochet products and materials. From raw yarns to handmade jackets, key chains, and mini bouquets, we craft each item with love and care.

Our mission is to inspire creativity and spread joy through the art of crochet. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned crafter, we're here to support your journey!

Our Story

Hooked On You began as a small passion project in the heart of Coimbatore. What started with a few skeins of yarn and a dream has grown into a beloved destination for crochet enthusiasts and craft lovers alike.

Our founder, a lifelong crochet artist, wanted to share the joy and therapeutic benefits of this beautiful craft with others. Today, we're proud to serve customers across India, bringing handmade warmth and creativity to homes everywhere.
